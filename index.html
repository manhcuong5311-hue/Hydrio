02
What Hydrio Does

Hydrio is a premium personal hydration tracking application for iOS that helps you:

  • Track daily water and beverage intake — water, tea, coffee, juice
  • Set personalized goals based on weight, gender, and activity level
  • Receive local on-device reminders to drink water
  • Monitor hydration history, streaks, and detailed statistics
  • Earn achievements and gamification rewards
  • Access an AI hydration coach with personalized daily insights
03
Medical Disclaimer
⚠ Important

Hydrio is not a medical application. Hydration recommendations are for general wellness purposes only and do not replace professional medical advice.

The goal calculations based on your weight, gender, and activity level are general estimates. Individual hydration needs vary significantly based on health conditions, climate, medications, and other factors.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding your hydration needs — especially if you have conditions affecting fluid intake such as kidney disease, heart failure, or diabetes.
